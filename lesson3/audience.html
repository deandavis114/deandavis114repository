John Thompson

This is John Thompson, he is a local ecological reasearcher in East Idaho. He lives in Idaho Falls and recently finished his graduate degree at Idaho State University in Pocetello. His reasearch is sensitive to the weather. The weather determines the amount and type of work he will be doing for the Forest Service.

Adam Thompson

Adam Thompson is John Thompson's younger twin brother. John is a telecom lineman. Weather often causes John to work late hours to keep the lights on. Knowing what the weather will be like helps John to plan his days so that he has more time with this family.
